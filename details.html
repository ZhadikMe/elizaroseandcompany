Every Eliza Rose & Co. canvas is fully customizable from start to finish. Choose one of seven background patterns — Double Stripes, Harlequin, Polka Dots, Stripes, Fleur De Lis, Small Damask or Large Damask — and pair it with one of six hand-mixed paint colors: Champagne, Ivory, Sahara, Taupe, Wheat or White. Then select a font from our collection of nineteen unique lettering styles, each named after a classic given name. Your child’s name, birth date, weight and length are hand-lettered directly onto the canvas to create a keepsake that is completely unique to your family. All canvases are printed on archival-quality materials and are built to last for generations. Ready to get started? Place your order and we will guide you through every step of the personalization process.
