Eliza Rose & Company specializes in handcrafted custom canvas art for newborns, babies and young children. Every piece is designed around your child’s name, birth details and your personal style preferences — from background patterns and paint colors to font selection. The result is a one-of-a-kind keepsake that grows with your family and becomes a treasured heirloom. Choose from elegant damask, playful polka dots, vintage stripes or bold harlequin patterns. Select from our curated palette of warm neutrals — champagne, ivory, taupe, sahara, wheat or white — and pair with one of nineteen hand-lettered fonts. Whether you prefer sweet and soft or bold and sassy, every canvas is crafted with care and shipped ready to hang. Browse the gallery, explore the details page, and then place your order today.
